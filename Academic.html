Keitsleigh: Excellence in Academic Achievement and Innovation

Keitsleigh is a leading institution renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation. Aftercare Services


At Keitsleigh, we provide dedicated aftercare services tailored to ensure the safety, comfort, and well-being of young children. Our experienced staff is committed to creating a nurturing and stimulating environment for every child.



  • Safe Environment:

    Secure and monitored facilities to ensure child safety at all times.

  • Nutritious Meals:

    Healthy and balanced meals prepared with care for growing children.

  • Creative Activities:

    Fun and engaging activities to enhance creativity and imagination.

  • Emotional Support:

    Warm and caring staff to provide emotional and social support.

  • Nap Time:

    Comfortable and quiet spaces for children to rest and recharge.

  • Early Learning Programs:

    Innovative educational programs designed to foster curiosity and a love for learning.
